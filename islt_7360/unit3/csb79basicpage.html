Tarantual Care

There really is not much need for a site on care for pet tarantula's, because there are quite a few out there, but as a keeper, the topic is of great interest to me. A lot of new tarantula owners are uninformed (as they make the impulsive decision to buy an exotic pet without researching) and this can cause serious harm to the tarantula. As Jamie's Tarantulas states:

Many, many tarantulas have been discarded, by uneducated owners when they are on their backs, about to molt.

So, the more information out there to help new owners, the better.
